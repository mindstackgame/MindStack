Common Topics

  • In-App Purchase Questions: Coin packs, No Ads Forever, or restore purchases.

  • Feature Requests & Feedback: Suggestions to improve gameplay or add new features.

Helpful Information

To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please include the following when contacting support:

  • Device Model: e.g. iPhone 15, iPad Air
  • iOS Version: Found in Settings → General → About
  • App Version: Found in Settings → General → About → Mind Stack
  • Description of the Issue: What happened and when
  • Screenshots: Attach if applicable — they help a lot!

For purchase-related refunds, please contact Apple Support directly at support.apple.com, as all payments are processed by Apple.
